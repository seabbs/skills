Create a UK news summary for today using BBC and Guardian sources.

## Phase 1: Headline Collection
1. Fetch top stories from BBC News UK, Guardian UK, BBC Politics, Guardian Politics

## Phase 2: Categorisation
2. Group by: breaking news, politics, economy, health/NHS, technology, climate/environment, other

## Phase 3: Deduplication and Summary
3. Combine perspectives for duplicate stories, note coverage differences
4. For each: headline, 2-3 sentence summary, key figures/data, both sources' perspectives

## Phase 4: Digest
5. Top 3 stories, brief summaries by category, ongoing stories with updates, links

Keep summaries factual and balanced. Highlight stories relevant to public health or data/modelling.
